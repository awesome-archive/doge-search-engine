Some Resources for CSCI 363

Request For Comments

Request for Comments (RFC) is a mechanism for the internet community to propose, revise, and distribute internet related protocols.

The official RFC documents are maintained by Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF).

  • http://www.ietf.org/rfc.html
